Design and Development of a 4-DOF Delta Robot

Introduction

This innovative project seamlessly combines Machine Learning and Android App Development to create a solution for plant disease detection, involving the training of a classification model and the development of an intuitive app for capturing and identifying plant diseases through image analysis.


Machine Learning

In the machine learning component of the project, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) were leveraged for image classification, with Python as the primary programming language and TensorFlow as the framework; the model was trained to accurately classify plant diseases based on images of plant leaves. The CNN architecture facilitated the learning of hierarchical features from the images, enabling precise predictions regarding the health status of the plants. The training process involved feeding the model with a diverse dataset of plant images representing various diseases. The CNN learned to recognize patterns and features indicative of different plant ailments. To enhance the model's performance, techniques such as data augmentation, transfer learning, and fine-tuning were employed.

Android Application

The Android application developed for this project serves as a user-friendly interface for plant disease detection and management. The app allows users to capture real-time images of plant leaves using the device's camera. These images are then processed through the pre-trained TensorFlow Lite model, enabling the app to swiftly and accurately identify any potential diseases affecting the plants. Upon detection, the Android app provides users with clear information about the identified plant disease. The interface not only displays the disease name but also offers valuable insights into the symptoms and potential consequences if left untreated. Furthermore, the application goes beyond simple diagnosis by providing users with recommended precautions and measures to mitigate the spread of the identified disease. This educational aspect empowers users with actionable steps to protect and maintain the health of their plants.
